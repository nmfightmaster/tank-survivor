# Free Sci-Fi Texture Pack Vol. 3

Thank you for downloading the third volume of my free sci-fi PBR texture pack!  
This pack contains 5 unique textures with a variety of surfaces including tiles, walls, panels, and organic tech.

## Contents:
- 5 seamless PBR textures
- Resolutions: 1K, 2K, 4K
- Formats: JPG and PNG
- Included maps:
  - Base Color
  - Normal
  - Roughness
  - Metallic
  - Ambient Occlusion
  - Height

Each texture is organized in folders according to format and resolution.
